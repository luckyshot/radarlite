RadarLite

A simple Android speed camera warning app with no ads, no subscriptions, no tracking, and no mobile data use while you drive.

RadarLite warns you when a known camera is ahead. The camera list comes from an open database maintained by thousands of collaborators, and once it is downloaded, alerts work offline.

Get the app Privacy policy

Features

  • Speed camera, red light camera, and average speed zone alerts.
  • Two warning levels: an early warning and a closer urgent alert.
  • Works offline after the camera database is installed.
  • Manual database updates when you choose to check.
  • No ads, subscriptions, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

How It Works

RadarLite listens for location updates already produced by your phone or navigation app. It does not start its own constant GPS polling.

When your route points toward a known camera, the app plays a warning sound and then an urgent sound as you get closer.

Quick Guide

  1. Install RadarLite on your Android phone.
  2. Open the app and grant the requested permissions.
  3. Tap Check for update to download the camera database.
  4. Turn monitoring on before driving.
  5. Use your usual navigation app if you want continuous location updates.

Privacy

RadarLite processes location on your device. It does not include ads, analytics, crash reporting, user accounts, or behavior tracking.

Internet access is used only when you manually check for a camera database update.

Important

Speed camera warning apps are restricted or illegal in some places. Check your local rules before using RadarLite on public roads.
